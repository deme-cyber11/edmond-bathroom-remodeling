Custom Shower Remodeling for Edmond Homes

Your shower is the most-used fixture in your bathroom, and it deserves to look and perform its best. Whether you are dealing with cracked grout, a leaking shower pan, or a builder-grade insert that has seen better days, our shower remodeling team at Scissortail Bath delivers custom solutions that combine beauty with lasting durability. We serve homeowners throughout Edmond, OK, from established neighborhoods near Coffee Creek and Oak Tree to newer communities around Deer Creek and Spring Creek.

Our licensed and insured crew specializes in custom tile showers, frameless glass enclosures, rain shower heads, handheld fixtures, and built-in niches that turn your daily routine into something you genuinely look forward to. Every shower we build starts with a properly sloped mortar bed and multi-layer waterproofing system to prevent leaks and moisture damage for decades. Our attention to detail extends to precise grout lines, sealed edges, and custom glass that fits your exact opening.

Tub-to-Shower Conversions

One of our most requested projects is the tub-to-shower conversion. Many Edmond homeowners have oversized bathtubs they rarely use, and converting that space into a spacious walk-in shower opens up the bathroom, adds modern appeal, and improves accessibility. We handle the plumbing relocation, waterproof membrane installation, custom tile work, and glass enclosure fitting as a single seamless project. The result is a dramatic transformation that makes your bathroom feel twice as large.

How We Approach Every Shower Remodel

A shower remodel is one of the highest-impact bathroom upgrades you can make. The shower is the most-used fixture in most bathrooms, and when it's done well — with quality tile, solid waterproofing, and fixtures that perform — it elevates the entire space. Scissortail Bath specializes in custom tile showers built to last, and we bring that same standard of craftsmanship to every project in the Edmond area.

We start with waterproofing. This is the step that separates long-lasting showers from ones that fail in a few years. We use industry-leading membrane systems to fully waterproof the shower pan, walls, and any niches or benches before a single tile goes up. This protects your subfloor, walls, and the rest of your home from moisture damage. Then we move to tile installation — using large-format tiles, mosaic patterns, subway tile, or whatever style fits your vision — with precision cuts, consistent grout lines, and a clean finish.

We install frameless glass enclosures, sliding doors, or open walk-in configurations depending on your layout and preference. Shower niches, built-in benches, and rainfall showerheads are all options we can incorporate. Every shower we build is fully code-compliant and comes with our craftsmanship warranty.
