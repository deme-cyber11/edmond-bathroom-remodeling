Professional Bathroom Tile Installation in Edmond

Nothing transforms a bathroom faster than expertly installed tile. From a classic subway tile backsplash to a dramatic floor-to-ceiling marble shower surround, tile defines the look and feel of your entire bathroom. At Scissortail Bath, our skilled tile installers work with every type of material, from budget-friendly ceramic and durable porcelain to luxury natural stone and hand-crafted artisan tile. We combine precise craftsmanship with proven waterproofing techniques to deliver tile work that looks stunning and lasts for decades.

Bathroom tile must do more than look good. It needs to withstand constant moisture exposure, daily foot traffic, and temperature changes without cracking, warping, or allowing water infiltration. That is why proper substrate preparation is just as important as the tile itself. Our licensed and insured team installs cement backer board, applies waterproof membranes in all wet areas, and ensures every surface is level and ready for tile before a single piece is set. We use premium thinset adhesives matched to your tile type and environment, and we grout with stain-resistant, mold-resistant formulas that maintain their appearance for years.

We install tile on bathroom floors, shower walls, shower floors, tub surrounds, accent walls, backsplashes, and bathroom niches. Popular options among Edmond homeowners include:

Professional Tile Installation: Precision Matters

Tile installation looks straightforward, but doing it right requires skill, patience, and the right materials. Uneven tiles, cracked grout, and failed waterproofing are all signs of work done without proper preparation. Scissortail Bath approaches every tile project — whether it's a shower wall, bathroom floor, or full room installation — with the same careful process that ensures a result that looks great and holds up for decades.

We begin with surface preparation. The substrate beneath the tile has to be flat, clean, and properly waterproofed in wet areas. Skipping this step is the most common reason tile installations fail prematurely. We use cement backer board in wet areas, apply waterproofing membranes where needed, and check the floor for levelness before any tile is set. This takes more time upfront, but it's what prevents callbacks and callbacks are something we take seriously.

Our tile setters work with large-format porcelain, natural stone, ceramic, glass mosaic, and everything in between. We handle pattern layouts, custom cuts around fixtures, and decorative borders with equal precision. Grout joints are consistent, grout color is matched to your design, and all joints are sealed for easy maintenance.
