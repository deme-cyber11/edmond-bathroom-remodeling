Bathtub Replacement in Edmond, OK

Upgrade to a luxurious soaking tub, freestanding centerpiece, or convert your tub to a modern walk-in shower.

Expert Bathtub Replacement for Edmond Homes

A worn, stained, or cracked bathtub drags down the entire look of your bathroom. Whether you want to swap your old tub for a luxurious soaking tub, install a stunning freestanding centerpiece, or convert the tub alcove into a spacious walk-in shower, the team at Edmond Bathroom Remodeling makes the transition smooth and stress-free. We handle everything from removal and disposal of your old tub to plumbing modifications, surround tile work, and final finishing so the result looks like it was always meant to be there.

Soaking tubs remain one of the most popular choices for Edmond homeowners looking to add a spa-like atmosphere to their master bathroom. We install deep soaking tubs in alcove, drop-in, and undermount configurations, pairing them with deck-mounted faucets and custom tile surrounds. For homeowners in neighborhoods like Coffee Creek, Oak Tree, and Spring Creek who want a true design statement, freestanding tubs deliver dramatic visual impact. We position them as the room's focal point, running new supply lines and drain connections to accommodate the standalone placement.

Tub-to-Shower Conversions

Tub-to-shower conversions are ideal for homeowners who rarely use their bathtub and would rather gain a large, accessible walk-in shower. We remove the existing tub, reframe the alcove as needed, install waterproofing membranes, build a custom tile shower with glass enclosure, and upgrade fixtures. This conversion opens up your bathroom layout, increases functionality, and often adds resale value to your Edmond home. We can also add bench seating, built-in niches, and grab bars for accessibility.

Bathtub Types We Install

  • Deep soaking tubs — Perfect for relaxation with deeper basins that allow full-body immersion.
  • Freestanding tubs — Elegant standalone designs that serve as the centerpiece of your bathroom.
  • Alcove and drop-in tubs — Classic configurations that fit standard openings with custom tile surrounds.
  • Whirlpool and air jet tubs — Therapeutic options with built-in massage jets for sore muscles and stress relief.
  • Tub-to-shower conversions — Remove the tub entirely and gain a spacious, modern walk-in shower.

What's Included in Every Bathtub Replacement

Our bathtub replacement service is comprehensive and covers every step of the process:

  • Old tub removal and disposal
  • Subfloor inspection and repair if needed
  • Plumbing modifications for new tub configuration
  • Custom tile surround installation
  • Deck-mounted or wall-mounted faucet and fixture installation
  • Caulking, grouting, and waterproof sealing
  • Final cleanup and walkthrough
