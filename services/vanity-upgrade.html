Custom Bathroom Vanity Upgrades in Edmond

Your bathroom vanity is the first thing you see when you walk into the room, and it sets the tone for the entire space. If your current vanity is outdated, too small, or lacking the storage your family needs, an upgrade can make a dramatic difference in both form and function. At Edmond Bathroom Remodeling, we design and install custom bathroom vanities that combine beautiful aesthetics with practical, everyday utility for homeowners throughout Edmond, OK and the greater OKC metro area.

Whether you are looking for a sleek floating vanity with clean lines for a modern master bath, a furniture-style double vanity with ample drawer space for a busy family bathroom, or a compact single-sink vanity for a guest bath near the UCO campus area, we build and install solutions tailored to your exact space and style. Our licensed and insured team handles everything from old vanity removal and plumbing modifications to countertop fabrication, sink installation, and final trim work.

Countertop Options

The countertop is the centerpiece of any vanity upgrade, and we offer a full range of premium materials to match your design vision and budget:

  • Granite countertops — Natural stone with unique veining and exceptional durability. Heat and scratch resistant, perfect for busy bathrooms.
  • Quartz countertops — Engineered stone that is non-porous, stain-resistant, and available in a wide range of colors and patterns. The most popular choice among Edmond homeowners.
  • Marble countertops — Classic luxury with distinctive veining for homeowners who want an elegant, high-end look.
  • Solid surface countertops — Seamless, easy to maintain, and available in hundreds of colors. Great for guest bathrooms and budget-conscious upgrades.

Vanity Styles and Configurations

We install vanities in every configuration to suit your bathroom layout and household needs:

  • Double-sink vanities — Eliminate morning bottlenecks with two sinks, ample counter space, and separate storage for each person.
  • Single-sink vanities — Ideal for guest bathrooms, powder rooms, and smaller spaces where efficiency matters.
  • Floating vanities — Wall-mounted designs that create a modern, airy feel and make floor cleaning effortless.
  • Furniture-style vanities — Freestanding pieces with legs and detailed woodwork for a traditional or transitional look.
  • Custom-built vanities — Built to your exact dimensions and specifications when off-the-shelf options do not fit your space.

What's Included in a Vanity Upgrade

Our vanity upgrade service is comprehensive and covers every detail:

  • Old vanity removal and disposal
  • Wall repair and paint touch-up behind the vanity
  • Plumbing modifications for new sink and faucet configuration
  • Countertop templating, fabrication, and installation
  • Undermount or vessel sink installation
  • Faucet, drain, and hardware installation
  • Mirror mounting and backsplash tile installation if desired
  • Final caulking, cleanup, and walkthrough
