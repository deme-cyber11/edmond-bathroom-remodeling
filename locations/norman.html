Bathroom Remodeling in Norman, OK

Quality bathroom renovations for Norman homeowners, from the OU campus area to established neighborhoods across Cleveland County.

Norman's Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Team

Norman is the third-largest city in Oklahoma and home to the University of Oklahoma, which means this community blends college-town energy with established residential neighborhoods full of families, professionals, and retirees. From the walkable charm of Campus Corner just north of the OU campus to the busy commercial corridor along Lindsey Street, Norman's character is as diverse as its housing stock. Whether you own a vintage bungalow near the OU area, a mid-century ranch in Brookhaven, or a newer home in the developments east of I-35, our team delivers bathroom remodels tailored to Norman's unique mix of architectural styles.

Many Norman homes near the university were built in the 1940s through 1970s, and their bathrooms show their age. Cast iron tubs with rust stains, cracked ceramic tile, single-bulb light fixtures, and cramped layouts are common in these older properties. Our team excels at reimagining these small spaces with smart design choices that maximize every square foot. We install custom walk-in showers where old tub-shower combos used to be, build floating vanities that create the illusion of more floor space, and use large-format porcelain tile to make bathrooms feel open and modern. For the newer homes in south and east Norman, we upgrade builder-grade finishes with custom tile work, quartz countertop vanities, and frameless glass enclosures.

Norman's strong housing market, driven in part by OU employment and Norman Public Schools, means a bathroom remodel is a solid investment in your property's value. We handle all City of Norman permits and inspections, and we work around your schedule to minimize disruption. Most projects are completed in two to four weeks. We also serve nearby communities including Moore, Oklahoma City, and Del City.
