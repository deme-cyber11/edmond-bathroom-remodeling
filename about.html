About Scissortail Bath

Locally Owned. Expertly Built.

Scissortail Bath is a locally owned and operated bathroom remodeling company proudly serving Edmond, Oklahoma and the greater OKC metro area. With years of hands-on experience in the industry, our team specializes in transforming outdated bathrooms into beautiful, functional spaces that homeowners love.

We are committed to quality craftsmanship on every project we take on — from simple cosmetic refreshes to full gut-and-rebuild remodels. As a licensed and insured contractor, we stand behind our work and treat every home as if it were our own.

Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional bathroom remodeling results with honest communication, fair pricing, and zero shortcuts. When you choose us, you get a dedicated team that cares about doing the job right the first time.
