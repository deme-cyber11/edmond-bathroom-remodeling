Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Scissortail Bath ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website scissortailbath.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our contact forms or request a quote. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Details about your bathroom remodeling project

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on each page.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and contact form submissions
  • Provide estimates for bathroom remodeling projects
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate and improve our website:

  • Form processing — Contact form submissions are transmitted securely to our internal lead management system. We do not share your submission with third-party marketing platforms.
  • Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about website traffic and user behavior. This helps us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. Google Analytics uses cookies to track interactions. You can learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity — We use Microsoft Clarity to analyze user experience through session recordings and heatmaps. Clarity helps us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve usability. You can learn more at Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytical data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies — Used by Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to collect usage data.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser settings or extensions
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
