Moore's Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Team

Moore is a resilient community that has rebuilt itself stronger after every challenge. That determination is reflected in the homeowners here who take pride in their properties and invest in making them better. Located along the I-35 corridor between Oklahoma City and Norman, Moore offers affordable housing, strong schools in the Moore Public Schools district, and quick access to employment centers across the metro. Our bathroom remodeling team is proud to serve Moore families with expert craftsmanship and honest pricing.

The housing stock in Moore ranges from established homes in Royal Park and Moore Central built in the 1970s and 1980s to newer developments in the Little River area and south Moore near 19th Street. Older Moore homes commonly feature outdated fiberglass tub-shower combos, worn laminate vanity tops, single-sink layouts, and inadequate ventilation that leads to moisture damage over time. Our team replaces these with modern porcelain tile floors and walls, custom walk-in showers with frameless glass doors, double vanities with quartz countertops, and proper exhaust systems that protect your investment for years to come. For newer Moore homes, we customize builder-grade bathrooms with upgraded finishes, heated flooring, and designer lighting that set your home apart.

Moore's affordable home prices make bathroom remodeling one of the highest-return investments you can make. A well-executed bathroom renovation can recoup a significant percentage of its cost at resale while dramatically improving your daily quality of life. We handle all City of Moore permits and inspections, maintain a clean and organized worksite, and complete most projects in two to four weeks. We also proudly serve neighboring communities including Midwest City, Del City, and Norman.
