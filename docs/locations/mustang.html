Mustang's Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Team

Mustang has transformed from a quiet rural town into one of the fastest-growing communities in the southwest OKC metro. Families are drawn here by the highly regarded Mustang Public Schools, affordable new construction, and a small-town feel that is increasingly hard to find close to the city. As the population has surged, so has the demand for home improvement services that match the quality expectations of Mustang homeowners. Our bathroom remodeling team meets that demand with expert craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and a commitment to getting your project done right the first time.

The housing stock in Mustang is diverse. Established neighborhoods in Mustang Valley and along State Highway 152 near Mustang Road feature homes built in the 1990s and early 2000s with bathrooms that are overdue for a refresh. Brass fixtures, cultured marble vanity tops, and basic fiberglass tub-shower combos are common in these properties. We replace them with modern porcelain tile, quartz countertops, brushed nickel or matte black hardware, and custom frameless glass shower enclosures that completely change the look and feel of the space. In Timber Ridge and Mustang's newer subdivisions south of SW 59th Street, we help homeowners upgrade builder-grade bathrooms with personalized touches like heated floors, LED vanity mirrors, and double-sink layouts.

Mustang's proximity to southwest Oklahoma City means you get big-city convenience with suburban peace and quiet. Our team is based in Edmond and serves the entire metro, so we are just a short drive away. We handle all City of Mustang permits, schedule inspections, and keep your project on a realistic timeline. Most bathroom remodels in Mustang take two to four weeks from demolition to final walkthrough. We also serve nearby communities including Yukon, Oklahoma City, and Moore.
