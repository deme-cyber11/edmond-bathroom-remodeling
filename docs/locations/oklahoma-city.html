Expert bathroom renovations serving OKC's finest neighborhoods, from Nichols Hills to Deep Deuce and beyond.

Oklahoma City's Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Team

Oklahoma City is the largest metro in the state, and its neighborhoods are as diverse as the homeowners who live in them. From the stately Tudor-style homes of Nichols Hills and the charming mid-century ranches of The Village to the historic bungalows of Mesta Park and the modern loft conversions in Deep Deuce, every OKC home deserves a bathroom that matches its unique character. Our team brings Edmond-based craftsmanship to every corner of the city, delivering custom bathroom renovations that look incredible and stand the test of time.

We have completed projects across Midtown OKC, where homeowners in restored bungalows and lofts near the Paseo Arts District want modern walk-in showers with subway tile and matte black fixtures. In Mesta Park, one of Oklahoma City's oldest and most prestigious neighborhoods, we specialize in preserving period charm while upgrading plumbing, lighting, and accessibility features. For families in the communities near the Lake Hefner area along Hefner Parkway and Memorial Road, we transform builder-grade bathrooms into personalized spaces with double vanities, heated tile floors, and frameless glass enclosures.

Oklahoma City homeowners face unique considerations like hard water mineral buildup and shifting clay soil that can affect plumbing lines. Our team understands these challenges and builds with materials and methods that account for local conditions. We manage all required City of Oklahoma City permits and schedule inspections, so you can focus on picking out your dream tile and fixtures while we handle the logistics. We also serve nearby areas including Edmond, Moore, Midwest City, and Del City.
