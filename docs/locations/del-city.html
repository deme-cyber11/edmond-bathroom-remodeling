Bathroom Remodeling in Del City, OK

Expert bathroom renovations for Del City homeowners, from the Sunnylane corridor to the quiet streets of Epperly Heights and beyond.

Del City's Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Team

Del City is a small, tight-knit community in the east OKC metro, sitting adjacent to Midwest City and just minutes from Oklahoma City proper. The city grew rapidly during the post-World War II and Korean War era, largely fueled by the expansion of Tinker Air Force Base directly to the east. That military heritage shaped the community and its housing stock in lasting ways. Generations of service members, defense workers, and their families have called Del City home, settling into neighborhoods like the Sunnylane area along the city's main commercial artery, the residential blocks lining the SE 29th Street corridor, the tree-lined streets of the Del Crest area, and the well-established homes of Epperly Heights. Today, Del City remains an affordable and convenient place to own a home in the metro, and our team is proud to help these homeowners modernize their bathrooms with quality craftsmanship they can count on.

The overwhelming majority of Del City's housing stock dates from the 1950s through the 1970s, and the bathrooms in these homes reflect the construction standards and design trends of that period. Galvanized steel plumbing that has corroded over the decades, cramped five-by-eight-foot layouts with a single overhead light, dated pink or green ceramic tile, and poor ventilation that has led to mold and moisture damage behind walls are issues we encounter regularly in Del City homes. Our remodeling team specializes in bringing these aging bathrooms into the modern era. We replace failing galvanized pipes with PEX during the renovation process, open up cramped layouts wherever possible, install custom tile walk-in showers to replace old tub-shower combos, and add proper exhaust ventilation to protect your investment for years to come. For homeowners looking to update without a full teardown, we offer targeted upgrades like new vanities with solid-surface countertops and modern floor and wall tile that transform the look and feel of the room.

With Tinker AFB as the largest single-site employer in Oklahoma, Del City sees a steady flow of military families and civilian defense workers looking for affordable homes close to the base. A freshly remodeled bathroom is one of the most impactful upgrades you can make, whether you are settling into a new home, preparing a property for resale, or simply improving the house your family has lived in for years. We handle all City of Del City permits and inspections, provide transparent written estimates before any work begins, and complete most bathroom remodels in two to three weeks. We also serve the surrounding communities of Midwest City, Oklahoma City, and Moore.
