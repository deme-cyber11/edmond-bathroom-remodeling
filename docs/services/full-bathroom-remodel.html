What's Included in a Full Bathroom Remodel

Our full bathroom remodel service covers every aspect of the renovation, giving you a completely refreshed space from floor to ceiling:

  • Demolition and Removal — Safe tear-out of old fixtures, tile, drywall, and flooring with thorough cleanup and debris hauling.
  • Plumbing Updates — Rerouting or replacing supply lines and drains, installing new fixtures, and ensuring code-compliant connections.
  • Electrical Work — Updated wiring, GFCI outlets, recessed lighting, exhaust fan installation, and heated flooring connections.
  • Tile and Flooring — Expert installation of ceramic, porcelain, or natural stone tile on floors, walls, and shower surrounds.
  • Fixtures and Hardware — Faucets, showerheads, toilets, towel bars, and accessories selected to match your design vision.
  • Vanity and Countertops — Custom or pre-built vanity installation with granite, quartz, or marble countertops.
  • Painting and Finishing — Moisture-resistant paint, trim work, caulking, and all the final details that make your bathroom shine.

Why Choose a Full Bathroom Remodel?

  • Increase Home Value — Bathroom renovations consistently rank among the top home improvements for ROI, often recouping 60 to 70 percent or more of the project cost at resale.
  • Modern Design — Replace dated tile, fixtures, and layouts with contemporary designs that make your bathroom feel like a retreat.
  • Improved Functionality — Reconfigure the layout to maximize storage, improve traffic flow, and add features like double vanities or walk-in showers.
  • Energy Efficiency — Upgrade to low-flow toilets, LED lighting, and energy-efficient exhaust fans to reduce utility bills.
  • Address Hidden Issues — A full remodel allows us to identify and fix water damage, mold, outdated plumbing, or faulty wiring behind the walls.
